Gym Exercises

  • Push-ups
  • Squats
  • Lunges
  • Plank
  • Jumping Jacks

Regular exercise keeps the body fit, strengthens muscles, improves flexibility, and boosts mental health. A combination of strength training, cardio, and stretching ensures overall fitness.

Diet Plan

  1. Breakfast: Oats & Eggs
  2. Lunch: Rice, Chicken & Veggies
  3. Snack: Nuts & Fruits
  4. Dinner: Salad & Paneer

A balanced diet is essential for staying healthy and energized. Eating a mix of proteins, carbohydrates, healthy fats, fruits, and vegetables helps the body recover faster, improves immunity, and supports muscle growth.

In conclusion, following a balanced diet along with regular exercise creates the perfect foundation for a healthy lifestyle. Together, they help maintain physical fitness, boost energy levels, and improve overall well-being in the long run.

Staying consistent with small daily habits is the key to long-term success. Even simple steps like drinking enough water, eating on time, and doing short workouts can bring big changes over time. Discipline and patience matter more than quick results.
